Airsoft Silencer

Although this silencer does not actually silence your gun, it does cover up the orange tip and make your AEG look fantastic!

So the first thing you will need is a piece of 1"od pvc pipe. Then use a handsaw, pipesaw, tablesaw, or whatever kind of saw you have, use it to cut the pipe to a 4" section. If you want to have it longer or shorter, that is up to you, but I recommend 4" inches for most guns. Anyways, take your section, and clamp it securely to the edge of a table or bench. Now comes the hard part.

Unfortunately, the 1"od pvc is not going to fit perfectly on the barrel of most AEGs, so you have to wear the inside down. There are two ways I found to do this. The first is to use a file to rub the inside of the section. The other way, and the way I recommend, is to use a drill to wear down the inside. Just keep wearing it down until it will fit securely on the end of your barrel. Now comes the fun part.

If you just use it as is, your silencer will not look good. Just white. What you need to do is spray paint it! As it will look the best on most guns, and it will look more realistic, I think you should use black, but you can use whatever color you want.

Congrats! You now have an airsoft silencer!
