Bipod

This will hold your gun up when you don't want to! This is perhaps one of the easiest diy ideas for airsoft I have seen anywhere.

All you need is some 5/8"od pvc pipe and a chip clip that has tublular handles. Cut it into two 8 3/8" lengths. Spray paint them black and fit them over the handles of the chip clip. Now you have a fuctioning bipod for your AEG.
